Care and Use:

  • - Do not use for any kind of twisting or prying.
  • - Do not use on frozen foods or hard objects.
  • - Hand wash only, do not use dishwasher.
  • - Do not strike a hard surface with the knife blade.
  • - Store your ceramic knife in a knife block.
  • - Always use on a bamboo, wood or plastic cutting board.
  • - Avoid cutting on marble or glass board.
  • - Avoid cutting bones, salt and ice.
